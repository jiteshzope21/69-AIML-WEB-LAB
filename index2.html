Chocolate Chip Cookies

A simple and delicious homemade cookie recipe

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making soft and tasty chocolate chip cookies. This recipe is easy to follow and perfect for beginners.


Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. Beat butter, sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.
  4. Add dry ingredients slowly to the wet mixture.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Place spoonfuls of dough on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8–10 minutes and let cool.
